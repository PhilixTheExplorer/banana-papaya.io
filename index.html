THE BANANA PAPAYA EXPERIMENT

Superman runs an experiment to measure two (2) quantities on the planet Krypton under different temperatures. One quantity has the unit of Bananas, and the other has the unit of Papayas. Superman discovers that both quantities are proportional to one another, and both of them are also proportional to the temperature, in Kelvin. He derives the following equation to express their relations:

B1 x P1 / K1 = B2 x P2 / K2

where B = Bananas, P = Papayas, K = Temperature in Kelvin and subscript, 1 and 2, denotes 2 different quantities. However, You can also find for celsius and fahrenheit.
